About NodeLoc

Shaping the future of technology education in Serbia

Our Mission

To provide world-class computer science education that empowers individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in the rapidly evolving technology industry. We are committed to fostering innovation, critical thinking, and practical expertise that prepares our students for successful careers in tech.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading technology education institution in the Balkans, known for producing highly skilled professionals who drive innovation and contribute to the global tech ecosystem. We envision a future where Serbia is a hub for technological excellence and our graduates are sought after by top companies worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, NodeLoc emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and the dynamic needs of the technology industry. What started as a small training center in Belgrade has grown into Serbia's premier computer science education institution, serving thousands of students and professionals.
